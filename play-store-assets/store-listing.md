## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Word Wheel is a pocket-size word puzzle. Drag across the letters on the
wheel to form words, fill the crossword above, and work your way through
100 progressively harder levels — all without ads, accounts, or an
internet connection.

★ HOW TO PLAY
• Drag from letter to letter on the wheel to build a word
• Drag back over the previous tile to undo the last letter
• Release your finger to submit — match a crossword answer to fill cells
• Type a word that isn't in the crossword? It still counts as a bonus
  and earns coins
• Tap the center of the wheel to shuffle the letters

★ FEATURES
• 100 progressive levels, from 4-letter starters up to 9-letter challenges
• Six difficulty tiers — Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert, Master, Legend
• Bonus word bank — every level has extra valid words beyond the crossword
• Daily spin wheel — free coins and hints every day you play
• Login streak counter — keep your streak alive across consecutive days
• Hint system — reveal a random letter whenever you get stuck
• Earn a free hint every 10 words you find
• Coins and hints carry across levels
• Resume button on the home screen — pick up exactly where you left off
• Portrait and landscape layouts — plays great on phones and tablets
• Responsive: cells and the wheel scale cleanly from small phones to
  10-inch tablets
• Sound effects (toggleable) for word found, bonus, hint reveal, and
  level completion

★ BUILT FOR FOCUS
No ads. No pop-ups. No subscription. No account required. No analytics
or tracking. The app doesn't send any data anywhere — everything stays
on your device.

★ OFFLINE
Works entirely offline. Perfect for flights, commutes, or anywhere you
want to give your brain a break without burning through mobile data.

★ OPEN SOURCE
Word Wheel is open source. You can read the source, file issues, or
submit improvements at:
https://github.com/hhariyanto29/word-wheels

★ TECHNICAL
• Native Android app built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose
• Small install — the full game is under 5 MB
• Works on Android 8.0 (Oreo) and up
• No native libraries, no extra downloads after install

★ PRIVACY
Word Wheel does not collect any personal information. There is no login,
no data sharing, no advertising SDK, and no analytics. Read the full
privacy policy at:
https://hhariyanto29.github.io/word-wheels/privacy-policy/

--

Have an idea for a new puzzle, an issue to report, or just want to say
hi? Open an issue on the GitHub repo linked above — all feedback is
welcome.

Good luck, and have fun spelling!
```
