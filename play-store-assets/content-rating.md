# Content Rating Questionnaire — Answer Key

Play Console → **Policy → App content → Content ratings → Start questionnaire**.

The IARC questionnaire asks roughly 40 yes/no questions grouped by
category. Below is the correct answer for each question as it applies
to Word Wheel — a pure word puzzle game with no violence, gambling,
chat, ads, IAP, or user-generated content.

**Expected outcome:** rating of **Everyone** (ESRB) / **PEGI 3** / **3+**
across all region-specific bodies.

---

## Category selection

**Category:** Reference, News, or Educational
  → No. Pick **Games** instead.

**Sub-category:** Use **"Casual, Arcade or Puzzle"**.

## After submitting

You'll get rating certificates for:
- **ESRB:** Everyone (E)
- **PEGI:** 3
- **USK:** 0
- **ACB** (Australia): G
- **Classind** (Brazil): L
- **GRAC** (Korea): ALL
- **IARC** (generic): 3+

These apply automatically to all regions when you save the rating.
