# Privacy Policy

**Word Wheel** ("the app") is developed and published by hhariyanto29.

**Effective date:** 2026-04-23
**Last updated:** 2026-04-23

This privacy policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

## Short version

**Word Wheel does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.**
Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

## What we do NOT collect

The app does not collect, request, or process any of the following:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone, address)
- Account credentials (the app has no login/account system)
- Payment or financial information
- Contacts, calendar entries, SMS, or call history
- Photos, videos, microphone, or camera
- Precise or approximate location
- Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, MAC address)
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Crash reports
- Any data sent to a remote server

## What stays on your device

The app stores a small amount of gameplay state **locally** on your device
so your progress persists between sessions:

- Current level number
- Coins and hints remaining
- Progress toward the next hint bonus

This data never leaves your device. It is removed if you uninstall the app
or clear its data from Android Settings.

## Permissions

The app does not request any special runtime permissions. It runs entirely
within the sandbox Android provides to every app and does not access
storage, contacts, location, camera, microphone, or network.

## Network access

The app does not make any network requests. It is fully playable offline
and has no online features.

## Third-party services

The app uses **no** third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising networks,
or crash reporting. It has no trackers.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including
children under 13. Because no information is collected at all, the app
is inherently safe for players of any age.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
will change and the new policy will be posted at the same URL. Material
changes will also be noted in the app's release notes on Google Play.
